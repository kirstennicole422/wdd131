Colorado Crochets

Site Name

I chose the site name Colorado Crochets (coloradocrochets.com) because it is alliterative, easy to remember, and related to the site topic.

Site Purpose

This site includes a reference page for crochet stitches, information about local meet ups, and a form to become a member.

Scenarios:

  • What stitches are good for beginners to learn?
  • When is the next crochet meetup near me?

Colors

I will use shades of blue and green for my color scheme. The main background and heading/section text will be light blue, the main text and heading/section background will be dark green and I will use medium blue and green as accents where needed.
