Colorado

Colorado is known as the "Centennial State" because it became a state in 1876, 100 years after the Declaration of Independence was signed. The blue and white stripes on the Colorado flag represent the vast blue skies and the snow capped mountains. The red of the C is from the origins of the word "Colorado" (meaning red colored) and the red rocks in the state. The golden circle represents the sunshine and gold that can be found throughout Colorado.
